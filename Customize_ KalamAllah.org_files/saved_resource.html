About Us

KalamAllah was established to assist learners of all levels who seek to learn Arabic and the Quran by providing them with instructors who cater to different levels. These instructors are hand-picked through a rigorous selection process ensuring they possess credentials necessary to teach Quran, Arabic and Islamic Studies.

Learn more

Mission

KalamAllah’s goal is to deliver excellent Arabic and Quran Teaching through online sessions that students can access online privately in the comfort of their own home.  KalamAllah strives to create a highly accessible online learning environment that allows students to master the science of learning Arabic and Quran efficiently and economically.

Learn more

Value

KalamAllah is designed to efficiently guide the learners to possess strong Islamic values and principles that the learners will proudly implement in everyday life. This program will help the learners deepen understanding of the message of the prophet Mohammad (PBUH). The learner, specially, must demonstrate eagerness and readiness to learn.

Offering Courses

ARABIC I,II,III

KalamAllah offers different level Arabic courses designed to advance skills focusing on reading, writing, and conversing. By the end of these courses, students should be able to understand, and speak Arabic proficiently. 

Learn more

TAFSEER

The Tafseer program consists of one-level classes led by distinguished instructors who will teach various interpretations and commentaries pertaining to the Quranic verses.

Learn more

AQEEDAH

The Creed (Aqeedah) course focuses on the fundamentals of Tawheed (Oneness of God), Belief in God, Angels, Holy Books, Prophets, Predestination and  Judgment Day. 

Learn more

FIQH

Fiqh Online sessions are designed to deepen the understanding of the jurisprudence and the Islamic law that derives its rules from Quran and Sunnah.

How We Operate

Step 1

The applicant will register online and select the plan that is the most suitable to their personal goals.

Step 2

A staff member will contact the applicant 24 hours after submitting the application to schedule an evaluation appointment and provide any additional information. 

Step 3

The applicant will be evaluated to determine level and appropriate placement. The applicant will also select desired class times and days.

Step 4

 The applicant will be assigned a teacher and class times that align with the applicant’s level and desired goals determined by the evaluation results.
